Commemoration of ASET@30

Home AES@30

Monday, 18th February 2013

1400Hrs-1830Hrs, Homi Bhabha Auditorium

Programme

Advances in Science, Engineering and Technology (ASET) at TIFR has taken its birth in the form of Instrumentation and Electronics (IAE) colloquia in March 1983. Need for such a forum where ideas and accomplishments in electronics and instrumentation could be discussed, was mooted in a meeting called by the then Group Committee V on February 18, 1983. IAE had its share of ups and downs and somewhere down its journey changed its name to ASET to reflect its wider appeal and identity.

To commemorate 30 years of ASET, a special event showcasing some of the advances in experimental techniques, detector and instrumentation development activities at TIFR is being organized. Some of the founding members of the ASET/IAE will participate in the event.

Day, Date and Time: Monday, 18th February 2013, 14.00 - 18.30 hrs
Venue: Homi Bhabha Auditorium, TIFR, Colaba, Mumbai, 400005

Programme:

2.00 - 2.30: Welcome, Inauguration and Reminiscences.
2.30 - 3.00: Mandar Deshmukh, DCMP&MS, Experimental tools and techniques to explore condensed matter systems.
3.00 - 3.30: Sudipta Maiti, DCS, Not just cheaper. Better.
3.30 - 4.00: N.K.Mondal, DHEP, Development of Detector & Instrumentation for High Energy Physics Research at TIFR.

4.00 - 4.30: High Tea

4.30 - 5.00: R.G.Pillay, DNAP, In-house developments for heavy ion accelerator facility.
5.00 - 5.30: Jaikumar Radhakrishnan, STCS, Trust and verify.
5.30 - 6.00: B.J.Rao, DBS, ASET-30 years: Biology Programme.
6.00 - 6.30: K. P. Singh, DAA, Astronomy from Outer Space at TIFR.


You are cordially invited to participate in this event.

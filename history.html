History of ASET

ASET has taken its birth in the form of Instrumentation and Electronics (IAE) colloquia in March 1983. Need for such a forum where ideas and accomplishments in electronics and instrumentation could be discussed, was mooted in a meeting called by the then Group Committee V on February 18, 1983. Regular IAE colloquia were held on every alternate Fridays soon there after. IAE had its share of ups and downs and somewhere down its journey changed its name to ASET to reflect its wider appeal and identity. The first volume of proceedings of the IAE colloquia held during 1983-84 were brought out by Profs. S.V.Damle, Dinesh Sharma and P.R.Apte, who were also mainly instrumental in starting and conducting major part of the IAE programme. Nothing is more appropriate than to reproduce the opening pages of this volume as the history of ASET. These include the Foreword by the former director of TIFR, Prof. B.V.Sreekantan, Preface by the chairman of the then Group Committee V, Prof. B.M.Udgaonkar and About This Issue by Profs. S.V.Damle, Dinesh Sharma and P.R.Apte. Relevance of this forum for TIFR is still unchanged from what was expressed by these pioneers two decades ago. To further instill nostalgia, these pages are reproduced here in their original form, including the now yellowish turned colour!
